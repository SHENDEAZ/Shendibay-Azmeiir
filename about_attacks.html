About Attacks to Networks

  Creating a fake base station, intercepting and decrypting transmitted data, exploiting vulnerabilities in communication protocols, password selection, attacks on client equipment and other methods that can violate network security. Here are a few attacks on our topic:

   1. Authentication attacks: This may include forging an ID or using stolen credentials to gain unauthorized access to the network.
   2. Data encryption attacks: this may include, the use of compromised encryption keys or attacks on encryption protocols that are used on the network.
   3.Access control attacks: This may include attempts to gain access to resources or services that are not allowed for a specific user.
